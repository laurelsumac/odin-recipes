Gochujang Cauliflower

Description

Delicious roasted cauliflower with gochujang sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 to 2 tbsp gochujang
  • 125g unsweetened Whole Milk Yogurt
  • Extra-virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 large cauliflower

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425*F
  2. Whisk together the gochujang and yogurt and add a drizzle of oil
  3. Remove the other leaves from the cauliflower and trim the stem so it is flush with the bottom of the cauliflower.
  4. Place the cauliflower on a sheet pan, drizzle with oil, and season well with sea salt and black pepper.
